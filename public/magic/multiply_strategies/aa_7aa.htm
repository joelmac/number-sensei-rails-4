(**) Adding Squared Numbers In The Form: a2 + (7a)2:

A.  This method is very easy since from algebra we know:

a2 + (7a)2 = 50a2

   B.  Therefore, you square the smaller number, then multiply by 50 for the answer (see section VI in Multiplying By Specific Numbers).

Ex [1]  62 + 422 =_________.

a)      62 = 36.

b)      36 x 50 = 1800.

c)      The answer is 1800.

Ex [2]  152 + 1052 =_________.

a)      152 = 225.  See Squaring a Number Ending in 5.

b)      225 x 50 = 11250.

c)  The answer is 11250.
