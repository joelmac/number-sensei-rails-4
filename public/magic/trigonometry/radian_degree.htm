Changing Radians to Degrees to Radians:

A.  In number sense it is imperative to understand how to convert from radians to degrees and vice versa.  

1.  Convert Radians to Degrees:

radians x 180/p

2.  Convert Degrees to Radians:

degrees x p/180

B.  Examples:

Ex [1]  225o = _________(radians)

a.  To convert to radians you should multiply by p/180.

b.  You should only focus on 225/180 and write B after the fraction is reduced.

c.  225/180 = 5/4.  

d.  The answer is 5p/4.

Ex [2]  5p/9 = __________(degrees)

a.  To convert to radians you should multiply by 180/p.

b.  Since the B's cancel each other, we should only focus on 5/9 x 180 .

c.  5/9 x 180 = 5 x 20 = 100.

c.  The answer is 100.
