(n-1)!

A.  This reduces to the following:

n(n - 1) + 1

  n - 1

B.  Examples:

Ex [1]  8! + 6! = _______

             7!

a.  The numerator of the answer is 8(7) + 1 = 57.

b.  The denominator of the answer is simply 7.

c.  The answer is 57/7.

Ex [2]  12! + 10! = _______

               11!

a.  The numerator is 12(11) + 1 = 133.

b.  The denominator is 11.

c.  The answer is 133/11.
