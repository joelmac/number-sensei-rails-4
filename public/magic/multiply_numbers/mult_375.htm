Multiplying By 375:

A.  Multiplying by 375 is derived from Multiplying by 125.  Since 375 = 1000 x (3/8) you can divide by 8, multiply by 3, and then multiply by 1000.

Ex [1]  168 x 375 =_________.

a)      168 ÷ 8 = 21.

b)      21 x 3 = 63.

c)      63 x 1000 = 63000.

d)      The answer is 63000.

Ex [2]  424 x 375 =_________.

a)      424 ÷ 8 = 53.

b)      53 x 3 = 159.

c)      53 x 1000 = 159000.

d)      The answer is 159000.
