Multiplying By 429:

   A.  Multiplying by 429 is derived Multiplying by 143.  Since 429 = 1001 x (3/7) you can divide by 7, multiply by 3, and then multiply by 1001.

Ex [1]  84 x 429 =_________.

a)      84 ÷ 7 = 12.

b)      12 x 3 = 36.

c)      36 x 1001 = 36036.

d)      The answer is 36036.

Ex [2]  847 x 429 =_________.

a)      847 ÷ 7 = 121.

b)      121 x 3 = 363.

c)      363 x 1001 = 363363.

d)  The answer 363363.
