Multiplying By 875:

   A.  Multiplying by 875 is derived from Multiplying by 125.  Since 875 = 1000 x (7/8) you can divide by 8, multiply by 7, and then multiply by 1000.

Ex [1]  88 x 875 =_________.

a)      88 ÷ 8 = 11.

b)      11 x 7 = 77.

c)      77 x 1000 = 77000.

d)      The answer is 77000.

Ex [2]  416 x 875 =_________.

a)      416 ÷ 8 = 52.

b)      52 x 7 = 364.

c)      364 x 1000 = 364000.

d)      The answer is 364000.
