Multiplying By 715:

   A. Multiplying by 715 is derived from Multiplying by 143.  Since 715 = 1001 x (5/7) you can divide by 7, multiply by 5, and multiply by 1001.

Ex [1]  28 x 715 =_________

a)      28 ÷ 7 = 4.

b)      4 x 5 = 20.

c)      20 x 1001 = 20020.

d)      The answer is 20020.

Ex [2]  714 x 715 =_________.

a)      714 ÷ 7 = 102.

b)      102 x 5 = 510.

c)      510 x 1001 = 510510.

d)      The answer is 510510.
