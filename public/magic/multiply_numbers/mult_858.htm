Multiplying By 858:

   A.  Multiplying by 858 is derived from Multiplying by 143.  Since 858 = 1001 x (6/7) you can divide by 7, multiply by 6, and multiply by 1001.

Ex [1]  91 x 858 =_________.

a)      91 ÷ 7 = 13.

b)      13 x 6 = 78.

c)      78 x 1001 = 78078.

d)      The answer is 78078.

Ex [2]  847 x 858 =_________.

a)      847 ÷ 7 = 121.

b)      121 x 6 = 726.

c)      726 x 1001 = 726726.

d)      The answer is 726726.
