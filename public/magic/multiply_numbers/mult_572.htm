Multiplying By 572:

   A.  Multiplying by 572 is derived Multiplying by 143.  Since 572 = 1001 x (4/7) you can divide by 7, multiply by 4, and then multiply by 1001.

Ex [1]  63 x 572 =_________.

a)      63 ÷ 7 = 9.

b)      9 x 4 = 36.

c)      36 x 1001 = 36036.

d)      The answer is 36036.

Ex [2]  721 x 572 =_________.

a)      721 ÷ 7 = 103.

b)      103 x 4 = 412,

c)      412 x 1001 = 412412.

d)      The answer is 412412.
